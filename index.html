Who am I ?

Hello, there and welcome to my profile, I am Assistant store manager at Bp currently working full-time.
juggling between work and learning to code,and looking forward to fresh change to endeavour on a
journey to become a full-stack developer.

applying for founder & coders:

Being an individual with a curiosity for Technology, never really thought to myself to make career out
a Tech industry as it set out only for the best minds which can be daunting, however after hearing lot of
success stories of people changing career from their everyday mundane jobs to a Software-developer.
This has inspired me to take the route to be a software Developer, which may seem like a challenging,
But I want to commit to prove those stories to be true see myself working a developer.
I believe the FAC Bootcamps has the best logically laid out program,
that helps anyone to develop the necessary skills required to be software developer.
